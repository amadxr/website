We’re delighted to announce that Paul Dobbins has joined the Resolve Digital team. Hailing from Houston, Texas, Paul and his wife Emily met in Philadelphia before moving to Stuart, Florida in 2012.

He started programming on an Apple IIe at age 8 and received his BS in Computer Science from Texas State University in 2002.

Paul is considered to be an exceptional developer by all who work with him. He’s been doing Ruby on Rails application development for over seven years and says: “Ruby on Rails is it for me.” Paul is the kind of guy who always goes the extra mile to ensure things are done right and deadlines are met. In that respect he fits in perfectly with our approach and has already become a valued member of our team.
