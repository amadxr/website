Development & Consulting Services Help You

  • Build & Extend Your Store

    When they come to us, most clients already have a store up and running. But they're often frustrated because it turns out the agency that was so great at design and branding isn't so great on the back-end. That's where our experience makes all the difference.

  • Optimize Your Store

    There are many ways to incrementally improve your site's performance. Using tools including Google Analytics, we improve the user experience and increase key performance indicators like average order size and conversion rate for web and mobile.

  • Manage Your Store

    Managing your online store can be a challenge, especially if you're also running a bricks-and-mortar business and have complex fulfillment and localization needs. We customize your store to match your unique business practices.

  • Maintain Your Store

    Even the most successful online stores experience technical issues and downtime. We minimize this by implementing security updates and upgrades to the Ruby, Rails and Spree or Solidus backend. And for your peace of mind we offer 24/7 support.
