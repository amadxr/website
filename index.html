Development & Consulting Services Help You

  • Build & Extend Your Store

    Most of our clients already have a store up and running when they come to us. But they're often frustrated. It turns out the agency that was so great at design and branding isn't so adept at the back-end. That's where our experience makes all the difference.

  • Optimize Your Store

    There are many ways to incrementally improve your site's performance. Using tools including Google Analytics, we improve the user experience and raise key performance indicators like average order size and conversion rate for web and mobile.

  • Manage Your Store

    Managing your online store can be a challenge, especially if you're also running a bricks-and-mortar business or have complex fulfillment and localization needs. We customize your store to match your unique business practices and work flow.

  • Maintain Your Store

    Even the most successful online stores experience technical issues and downtime. Our job is to minimize disruptions by applying security updates and upgrades to the Ruby, Rails and Spree or Solidus backend. And for your peace of mind we offer 24/7 support.
