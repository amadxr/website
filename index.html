Development & Consulting Services

Many clients already have an online store when they come to us. But they're dissatisfied because the agency that was great at design and branding isn't necessarily adept at back-end development or focused on ROI. That's where our skill and experience makes all the difference.

Build

Setting up your online store correctly can be a challenge, especially if you're running a bricks-and-mortar business or have complex fulfillment or localization requirements. We customize your store to match your unique business practices and workflow.

Optimize

We work to incrementally boost your site's performance. Using analytics and Machine Learning, we strategically improve the customer experience for web and mobile. This raises key performance indicators like average order size and conversion rate.

Maintain

Even the most successful eCommerce sites experience technical issues. Our job is to minimize costly disruptions by rigorous monitoring and security updates. We also perform upgrades to the Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Spree and Solidus.
