Development & Consulting Services

Many clients already have an online store when they come to us, but they're often frustrated and disappointed. The agency that was great at design and branding isn't necessarily adept at the back-end, or focused on ROI. That's where our skill and experience make all the difference.

Build

Setting up your online store correctly can be a challenge, especially if you're running a bricks-and-mortar business or have complex fulfillment or localization needs. We customize your store to match your unique business practices and workflow.

 

Maintain

Even the most successful eCommerce sites experience technical issues. Our job is to minimize disruptions by rigorous monitoring and security updates. We also perform upgrades to the Ruby, Rails, and Spree or Solidus backend.

Optimize
We work with you to incrementally improve your site's performance. Using tools including Google Analytics and Algolia Search, we improve the user experience and raise key performance indicators like average order size and conversion rate for web and mobile.
