--- layout: post title: 'Ace: Evolution of a Game Character' ---

Ace Rider is a motorcycle game for iOS that we released two years ago. The game's character - Ace - remained anonymous for that game, concealed within his helmet like a bobble-headed Stig. His anonymity made him more relatable.

With our new game, Ace Flyer, we realized Ace had to reveal his identity. So who is Ace? What does he look like? Turns out he's a dashing young chap with a taste for speed and adventure.

Early concepts

Flying goggles were a requirement. No self respecting air racer would take to the skies without them. They also allow Ace to retain a degree of anonymity.

Early on we were thinking he'd have a mop of tousled hair that would be blown back in the wind during a race:

...but the character is so small in the game play view that his hair wouldn't be clearly visible. Instead, Ace wears a classic leather flying helmet. He'll also be able to upgrade his headgear to some pretty awesome alternatives.

And here he is - drum roll please!
