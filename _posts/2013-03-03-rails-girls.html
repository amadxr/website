Rails Girls is a non-profit volunteer community with the aim to introduce women to technology and programming through learning Ruby on Rails. Originally started in Finland, it is now a global organisation.  

A local programmer Rebekah Wilson organised the Christchurch Rails Girls event which was held last weekend in a local school. There were 24 women ranging in age from 9 to 40+ and 10 coaches from the local Ruby community. Two of us from Resolve Digital came along to help out. It is fascinating how challenging it is to convey programming concepts.  Just what is a variable anyway?! ;-)

By the end of the weekend there were 24 new Heroku sites and a number of new recruits to the Ruby on Rails community. There will be follow up events to give the new programmers all the support and encouragement we can.
