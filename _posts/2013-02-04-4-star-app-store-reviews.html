--- layout: post title: 4 star App Store reviews ---

Reviews on your app can be a mixed bag. How do you find useful feedback from thousands of reviews? Especially when so many reviews are often just one or two words to pass Apple's review validation requirements.

With our app Ace Rider, I find 4 star reviews to be the most telling. 4 star reviews are often players who understand and respect our app but there are one or two things which bother them so they hold a star back from you. Best of all, they tell you why.

Here's a sample of Ace Rider's 4 star reviews:

★★★★☆ Good game needs better controls
This game is good but the controls are a little bit hard to use. Improve the controls and it will b a 5 star game.

★★★★☆ Good game. If u added a brake it be even better
I get that it makes it more difficult without a brake but at the same time less fun also

★★★★☆ Great fun.
Easy, awesome. Any more levels on the horizon?

★★★★☆ Fun
Good game but you should be able to tilt in both directions

★★★★☆ Woohoo
Super fun but it would be more fun if it had brakes and if leaning your device would make your rider lean.
