--- layout: post title: Welcome Tim! ---

We're very happy to announce that Tim Ross has joined the Resolve Digital team in our Christchurch office.

Tim taught himself programming on the Commodore 64 at the tender age of 9 and created his first web site at 16. He began his studies in Computer Science at the University of Canterbury but his passion for web design led him to the National College of Design and Technology where he completed the first web technology course. In 2000 he starting working as a web developer.

In 2006, like many young kiwis, Tim moved to London with his girlfriend (now wife) to explore Europe and further his career, working as a contract .NET developer for clients such as the BBC, uSwitch.com, ITV and Vodafone.

Moving back to New Zealand in 2011, Tim co-founded elucidcode, a mobile app development company. He brings a wealth of experience to Resolve having developed iOS apps for clients including the NZ Ministry of Economic Development, 7digital, Jade Software and Douglas Pharmaceuticals.
