Thanks to Gap Filler, the people of Christchurch, New Zealand have a fun way of contributing to the city’s post-quake future. Their site-specific projects inspire experimentation, imagination, and play.

With a dozen or more temporary projects at any given time, Gap Filler had difficulty maintaining and publishing an accurate list. Their printed “Gap Map” provided limited information and was quickly outdated.

We solved this problem with an easy to use iOS app that allows Gap Filler to create and promote up-to-the-minute listings and events. Find out more about the app or download the press release (PDF).
