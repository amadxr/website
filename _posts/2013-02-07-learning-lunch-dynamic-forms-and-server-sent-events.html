Keeping up with the quickly changing tech world is difficult. To help, every week we have a Learning Lunch where we pick out a video to watch as a group and discuss while we eat. These will often be videos of conference talks or from one of the many excellent Ruby on Rails screencasters.

This week we fit in two episodes from the seminal RailsCasts. One, on dynamic forms, we thought could be useful for a project we're currently working on and the other, on server-sent events, exposed us to a new feature of the upcoming major version of Rails. Both were densely packed with information.
