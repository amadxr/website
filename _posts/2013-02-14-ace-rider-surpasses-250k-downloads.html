--- layout: post title: Ace Rider surpasses 250k downloads ---

Today we reached an important milestone for Ace Rider: 250,000 downloads. While this doesn't put Ace Rider in the ranks of the top games in the App Store, it does represent a significant achievement. 

Perhaps the best part is it has received over 1700 ratings with an average of 4.5 stars (out of 5 max). We're really proud of that. It tells us we've created a quality game people in over 100 nations around the world are playing and enjoying. 

And that's inspiring us to forge ahead with our next challenge for Ace... stay tuned.
