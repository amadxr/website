--- layout: post title: Ace Flyer demo at The Big Indie Pitch ---

David Jones and Patrick Morrow demo'd Ace Flyer for the first time at The Big Indie Pitch in San Francisco. Ace was previewed by Touch Arcade, TUAW, 148Apps, IGN and many more leading journalists, bloggers and producers.

It was done speed dating style: each pitch lasting just 5 minutes. With adrenaline flowing, this was a unique opportunity to meet some great people and show off our latest iOS game (still in development but coming along nicely).
