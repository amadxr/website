--- layout: post title: Ace Rider demo at Apps World ---

Yesterday we did a demo of Ace Rider at the iOS Developers & Entrepreneurs mixer, hosted at Apps World at the Moscone Center.

Often as designers and developers we operate in a bit of a vacuum - we don't get to see people using or experiencing our work. So it was great to have an opportunity to see some real world reactions to the game. And, happily, the reactions were positive!
