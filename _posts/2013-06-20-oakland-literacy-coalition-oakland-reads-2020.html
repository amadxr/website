--- layout: post title: Oakland Literacy Coalition & Oakland Reads 2020 ---

The Oakland Literacy Coalition is launching the Oakland Reads 2020 campaign to ensure that all Oakland children are reading at a proficient level by the end of third grade.

They presented us with an interesting challenge: create two sites which have a similar look and feel but address the needs of very different audiences.

The Coalition website allows organizations to sign up to be part of a network of literacy service providers and advocates. Members can login to maintain their own directory listing and learn about upcoming meetings and events.

The OR 2020 site is helping inform parents, teachers, policy makers and the general public about the campaign and inspire them to join the effort.

We were pretty happy about this email from our client shortly after the sites launched:

Here's a comment I received from one of the literacy coalition members: "Nice! Congrats to everybody who helped develop these immensely appealing and informative websites. Well done!"

It's been great working with the Resolve team and we are all so pleased with the outcome. Thank you all!
